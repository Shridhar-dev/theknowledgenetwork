About Us

One of the issues faced by the medical research professionals in India today is that it is difficult to find other researchers working in the same domain.
Non-availability of information makes it difficult for the researchers to collaborate & work together. We provide you an effective solution to overcome this barrier.
Once a User enters a query, our website scans the PubMed database and identifies experts through high impact publications and number of citations. It thus makes it easy to find other professionals working on the same topic..
